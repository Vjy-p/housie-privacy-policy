Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Housie Game ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using the application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information while you use the app:

  • Anonymous user ID created through Firebase Anonymous Authentication.
  • Your display name (nickname) entered in the app.
  • Your optional profile picture, if you choose to upload one.
  • Gameplay information such as rooms, scores, wins, achievements and leaderboard data.
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information.
  • Analytics information used to improve the app.
  • Advertising identifiers used by Google AdMob.

Anonymous Authentication

Our app uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to create a secure anonymous account. You are not required to create an account using an email address, password or phone number. This anonymous account allows us to save your game progress and profile securely.

Profile Information

You may choose a display name and optionally upload a profile picture. Your display name and profile picture may be visible to other players in multiplayer rooms and on leaderboards.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide online multiplayer gameplay.
  • Save your game progress.
  • Display leaderboards and player rankings.
  • Improve app performance and stability.
  • Prevent abuse, cheating and fraudulent activity.
  • Display advertisements using Google AdMob.
  • Analyze app usage to improve gameplay and user experience.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and other information to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand how the app is used, identify crashes, improve performance, and provide a better gaming experience.

Notifications

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) may be used to send important notifications related to gameplay, updates or announcements.

Data Storage

Game data, anonymous account information, display names and optional profile pictures are securely stored using Firebase and Supabase services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your information may be processed only by trusted service providers that help operate our application, including:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Analytics, Crash Reporting and Notifications)
  • Supabase (Database and File Storage)

Children's Privacy

Housie Game is intended for a general audience. Children should use the app under the supervision of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information beyond what is necessary to provide the gameplay experience.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures and encrypted connections to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

You may delete your account and associated game data at any time using the Delete Account option available within the app. If you are unable to access the application, you may request deletion by contacting us using the email address below.
