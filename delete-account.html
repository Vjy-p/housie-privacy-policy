Delete Account

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

Housie Game allows you to permanently delete your account and associated data at any time. You can complete this process directly within the app without contacting support.

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Housie Game app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Read the confirmation message.
  5. Tap Delete to permanently remove your account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Your anonymous account.
  • Your display name.
  • Your profile picture.
  • Your game profile.
  • Your leaderboard information.
  • Your gameplay statistics.
  • Your game history stored in our database.

Data That May Be Retained

Certain anonymous information may be retained for a limited period where required for:

  • Fraud prevention.
  • Security investigations.
  • Legal obligations.
  • Anonymous analytics.
  • Crash reporting.

These records do not personally identify you.
